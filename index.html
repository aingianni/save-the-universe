Manage your shields and ship health to take out the enemy.

  • If your shields fall below "50" you will start taking half damage, half shield.
  • Once your shields are depleted, they can no longer be repaired.
  • Your attack deals between 80 percent and 120 percent of your base damage.
  • The enemy attacks follow the same logic.
  • Your successful attacks will build to a charged super attack at "10", this attack deals 50 damage.
